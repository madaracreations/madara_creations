How To Order

1. Choose Your Design

Browse through our collection and select the design you love

2. Share Your Custom Details

Get the required information from our website and send us a WhatsApp message with all your customization preferences (colors, images, wordings, etc...)

3. Confirm Your Order

We will confirm your order details. Full payment is required to proceed with production

4. Production Time

• Normal resin crafts: 2 weeks delivery time after payment
• Flower preservation items: 6–8 weeks after full payment

5. Order Tracking

You can track your order status anytime through our website

6. Safe Delivery

Your handcrafted item will be carefully packed and delivered safely to your address. We will also inform you before the delivery is made
